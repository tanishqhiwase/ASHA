E-Health Card

Gain access to your personalized E-Health card, a digital gateway to your medical records and healthcare services.

  • Health Number: Your unique health identifier, facilitating quick and efficient access to your medical history and records.
  • Prescription: Access your prescriptions digitally, ensuring convenience and accuracy in medication management.
  • Personal Information: Store essential details such as your name, photo, and other pertinent information securely.
  • Diagnosis Updates: Stay informed with real-time updates on your diagnosis and treatment progress.

Your E-Health card also contains brief information about the hospital and the attending doctor. This ensures that you are well-informed about the healthcare professionals involved in your diagnosis and treatment process.

Government Health Schemes

Learn about various government health schemes, including eligibility criteria and application procedures, to maximize healthcare benefits.

  • Universal Health Coverage: Provides access to essential health services without financial hardship.
  • Maternal and Child Health Programs: Ensures the well-being of mothers and children through various healthcare interventions.
  • Immunization Programs: Protects individuals from vaccine-preventable diseases through timely vaccinations.
  • Nutrition Programs: Promotes good nutrition and healthy eating habits to prevent malnutrition and related health issues.
  • Disease Control Programs: Focuses on controlling and preventing the spread of communicable diseases such as malaria, tuberculosis, and HIV/AIDS.

Health Tracking Service

Track your health progress and get insights into your wellness journey with our health tracking service.

  • Personalized Health Goals: Set and monitor your health goals, such as weight management, fitness targets, and dietary improvements.
  • Activity Tracking: Keep track of your physical activities, including steps taken, distance traveled, and calories burned.
  • Health Metrics: Monitor vital health metrics such as blood pressure, heart rate, blood sugar levels, and sleep patterns.
  • Progress Reports: Receive regular reports and insights on your health progress, highlighting areas of improvement and potential concerns.
  • Health Education: Access educational resources and tips on maintaining a healthy lifestyle, managing chronic conditions, and preventing diseases.
